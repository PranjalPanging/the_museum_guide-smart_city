# THe Museum Guide - Smart City

## Description

The Museum Guide is a smart cultural discovery and journey-tracking platform designed to help users explore Indiaâ€™s rich museum heritage in one centralized place. The platform not only allows users to discover museums across India but also helps them track their cultural journey, record experiences, and receive AI-powered insights to enhance their visits.

Users can sign up and log in securely to access a personalized dashboard where their entire museum journey is visualized. The application combines discovery, memory-keeping, and AI assistance to make museum exploration more engaging and meaningful.

## Features
### Authentication
Secure Login and Signup
Personalized user accounts

### Dashboard
Overview of visited museums
Wishlist museums
Journey statistics and progress tracking

### Journey Tracking
Mark museums as Visited
Add museums to Wishlist
Track visit dates and personal notes
Timeline view of the cultural journey

### Reviews and Experiences
Rate visited museums
Write personal reviews and reflections

### AI-Powered Features
AI Museum Summary
Generates concise and informative summaries of museums
Helps users quickly understand cultural and historical significance

### AI Scanner
Scan museum exhibits or descriptions to receive instant explanations and insights
### AI Suggestions
Personalized museum recommendations
